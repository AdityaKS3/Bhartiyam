GENESIS

BHARTIYAM was established in the year 1998 as a Non-Government Organization (NGO) by a group of committed social and cultural activists and professionals to put sincere efforts for the elimination of poverty, inequality and injustice from the society. Bhartiyam is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 on 22nd December 1999.



VISION

To establish a progressive, peaceful and cultural society based on the values of equality, fraternity and mutual-help.







MISSION

To educate organize and empower the rural poor by promoting development as a liberating force for achieving social justice, economic growth and self-reliance.
