ABOUT US


Bhartiyam is one of the premier Non-Government Organizations in Bihar established in 1985. Since establishment, it is working with the marginalized and the deprived communities mainstreaming them with economic, social and cultural development, improving their living status, health status, education, leadership skill, entrepreneurship and sustainable livelihood options.

It was founded by a group of dedicated and committed social and cultural activists, who were inspired by the ideologies of Mahatma Gandhi and Jai Prakash Narayan to work for the creation of a just and equal society.

It gradually moving to enhancement of cultural activity, knowledge, skill, expertise to advance business portfolio, get access to health services, ensure gender and social equity, strengthen local governance, have access to government schemes, conserve natural resources, mitigate calamities and dialogue with stakeholders/ policy makers for making them accountable to pro poor policies. Constant effort over 26 years brought significant improvement among thousands of families (women & children in particular) through developmental projects.
